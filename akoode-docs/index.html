Case Study: Digital Transformation of Mullen Equipments by Akoode Technologies

Company Highlight - 75+ Years in the business

Client Overview

Mullen Equipments is a trusted name in industrial machinery and tools manufacturing, serving clients across industries like construction, mining, and heavy industries. With over three decades of experience, the company is known for its commitment to quality. However, they faced challenges in embracing the digital era, where efficiency, data-driven decisions, and superior customer experiences are vital to remain competitive.

Challenges

Mullen Equipments approached Akoode Technologies to tackle several critical issues:

  • Outdated Systems: Their legacy ERP and CRM systems operated in silos, making it difficult to access real-time, company-wide data.
  • Manual Processes: Core functions such as inventory management, order tracking, and customer support were manually handled, leading to inefficiencies and errors.
  • Limited Online Presence: Their outdated website lacked modern features to attract and engage customers effectively.
  • Data Insights: Without a robust analytics framework, decision-making and forecasting were challenging.
  • Customer Engagement: The company needed tools to provide better post-sales support and personalized customer experiences.
Solution Delivered by Akoode Technologies

Akoode Technologies collaborated closely with Mullen Equipments to design and execute a digital transformation plan tailored to their unique needs. The project included:

  • Cloud-Based ERP System: Implemented a custom cloud-based ERP system with a seamless Node.js backend and a user-friendly React.js frontend. This integration unified inventory, sales, and procurement processes. Automated workflows, eliminating redundancies and reducing errors.
  • Mobile Application Development: Built a cross-platform mobile application using Flutter to enable real-time inventory tracking, order management, and better coordination among field technicians.
  • Website Redesign: Revamped the company's website with a modern, responsive design using a custom WordPress solution. Added AI-driven content recommendations to help users find relevant products and services quickly.
  • AI-Driven Analytics: Deployed an advanced analytics platform to deliver actionable insights on sales trends, customer behavior, and supply chain efficiency. Introduced predictive maintenance capabilities, reducing machinery downtime for their clients.
  • Customer Engagement Portal: Developed an easy-to-use customer portal for tracking orders, submitting service requests, and accessing product documentation. Integrated a chatbot powered by natural language processing (NLP) to offer 24/7 customer support.
Results Achieved

The transformation delivered measurable benefits for Mullen Equipments:

  • Operational Efficiency: Automated processes reduced manual efforts by 60%, speeding up order processing and inventory updates. Centralized data access improved inter-departmental collaboration.
  • Enhanced Customer Experience: The redesigned website and mobile app increased customer engagement by 70%. The customer portal and chatbot reduced query resolution time by 40%.
  • Improved Decision-Making: AI-powered analytics enhanced demand forecasting accuracy by 15%. Predictive maintenance insights minimized client machinery downtime by 20%.
Testimonial

“Akoode Technologies has transformed our operations and customer experience. Their innovative solutions have streamlined processes, improved engagement, and set us up for long-term success. We’re thrilled with the results and look forward to our continued collaboration.” — Eddie Kmit, CEO, Mullen Equipments

Conclusion

Akoode Technologies successfully modernized Mullen Equipments’ operations and customer interactions, ensuring the company remains competitive in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. This collaboration highlights Akoode Technologies’ expertise in delivering customized digital transformation solutions that drive tangible results.

Transforming Franchise Management for Dapper Doughnuts

Company Highlight - 2 M + Order Served

About the Client

Dapper Doughnuts is an international gourmet doughnut franchise celebrated for its fresh mini doughnuts and creative flavors. With franchises spanning multiple countries, Dapper Doughnuts has rapidly grown into a household name. However, scaling their operations exposed challenges in maintaining consistency, streamlining processes, and providing franchisees with the tools needed to succeed.

The Challenge

Dapper Doughnuts’ CEO, Danny Grammenopoulos, recognized the need for a robust digital solution to address several pain points:

  • Disconnected Operations: The absence of a centralized system led to inconsistent communication, reporting, and operational inefficiencies.
  • Cumbersome Onboarding: Franchisee onboarding relied heavily on manual processes, delaying operational readiness.
  • Lack of Real-Time Insights: Monitoring key metrics like sales, inventory, and staff performance across all franchises was challenging.
  • Insufficient Franchisee Tools: Franchisees struggled with day-to-day management due to limited access to resources and support.
Akoode Technologies’ Tailored Solution

Akoode Technologies collaborated with Dapper Doughnuts to design a cutting-edge franchise management system, addressing each of their challenges with innovative technology. Key components included:

  • Centralized Franchise Management Platform: Developed a cloud-based platform with a scalable Node.js backend and a React.js frontend, consolidating franchise operations into a single hub. Streamlined data access, enabling the corporate team to manage and monitor franchise performance effortlessly.
  • Simplified Onboarding Workflow: Automated onboarding processes, reducing paperwork and accelerating new franchisee setup. Integrated interactive training modules directly into the platform, ensuring new franchisees are operational faster.
  • AI-Powered Analytics: Built intuitive dashboards to provide real-time visibility into key performance indicators (KPIs) such as sales trends, inventory levels, and staff productivity. Empowered corporate and franchisees with actionable insights to make data-driven decisions.
  • Comprehensive Franchisee Portal: Created a franchisee portal with tools to manage inventory, track sales, and access operational resources. Introduced an AI-driven chatbot for 24/7 support, addressing common queries and troubleshooting issues.
  • Mobile-First Convenience: Designed a mobile app using Flutter, offering franchisees on-the-go access to critical management tools. Features included real-time sales tracking, staff scheduling, and direct communication with corporate.
The Results

The solution delivered by Akoode Technologies transformed franchise management for Dapper Doughnuts, leading to measurable improvements:

  • Operational Excellence: The centralized system cut administrative overhead by 40% and improved operational efficiency across all franchise locations. Automated onboarding reduced setup time for new franchisees by 50%.
  • Enhanced Franchisee Satisfaction: User-friendly tools and on-demand resources empowered franchisees to operate with greater confidence and efficiency. Franchisee satisfaction scores improved significantly, reflecting the ease of use and accessibility of the new system.
  • Smarter Business Decisions: AI-powered insights improved demand forecasting accuracy and enabled the corporate team to identify and address performance issues proactively.
  • Scalability for Growth: The flexible architecture ensures that new franchise locations can be integrated seamlessly, supporting Dapper Doughnuts’ expansion goals.

The team’s expertise and commitment to innovation have been invaluable.” — Danny Grammenopoulos, CEO, Dapper Doughnuts

Case Study: Revolutionizing Player Analytics with Computer Vision for Football

Company Highlight - Coach of American Football Team

About the Client

A professional football analytics firm approached Akoode Technologies to develop an advanced player tracking system. Their goal was to harness video footage to provide deeper insights into player performance, focusing on speed, acceleration, and movement angles. Traditional methods of data collection were manual and time-consuming, limiting the depth and speed of insights provided to teams and coaches.

The Challenge

The client faced several hurdles that required an innovative solution:

  • Manual Data Processing: Existing methods relied heavily on manual tagging and analysis of player movements, which were error-prone and time-intensive.
  • Real-Time Insights: Coaches and analysts needed faster access to actionable insights during and after games.
  • Accuracy and Precision: Capturing and calculating player metrics such as speed, acceleration, and angles from dynamic video footage posed significant technical challenges.
  • Scalability: The solution needed to process large volumes of video data across multiple games without compromising performance.
Akoode Technologies’ Solution

Akoode Technologies designed and implemented a state-of-the-art computer vision system to meet these challenges. Key features included:

  • AI-Powered Player Detection: Utilized deep learning models to detect and track players in real time from video footage. Integrated pre-trained models for object detection, customized for identifying football players under varying lighting and field conditions.
  • Speed and Acceleration Measurement: Developed algorithms to calculate player speed and acceleration using frame-by-frame positional data. Incorporated advanced techniques to account for camera perspective distortions, ensuring precise measurements.
  • Movement Angle Analysis: Implemented algorithms to calculate movement angles, enabling insights into player direction and agility. Provided visual overlays for better interpretation during video reviews.
  • Scalable Video Processing: Built a scalable architecture capable of processing multiple video streams simultaneously. Leveraged cloud-based computing to handle high workloads during peak game seasons.
  • Real-Time and Post-Match Insights: Designed a dashboard to display real-time metrics for coaches during games. Enabled post-match analysis with detailed player performance reports and visualizations.
Results Achieved

The computer vision solution delivered remarkable results for the client:

  • Improved Efficiency: Reduced data processing time by 70%, allowing analysts to focus on strategy rather than manual data entry.
  • Enhanced Accuracy: Achieved a 95% accuracy rate in detecting players and measuring performance metrics, surpassing manual methods.
  • Real-Time Analytics: Delivered live insights during games, empowering coaches to make data-driven decisions on substitutions and tactics.
  • Scalability for Growth: Processed video data from multiple games simultaneously, supporting the client’s expanding operations.
  • Actionable Insights: Provided detailed reports highlighting key metrics such as top speed, average acceleration, and directional changes, giving teams a competitive edge.
What the Client Says

“Akoode Technologies’ computer vision expertise has transformed the way we analyze player performance. The speed, accuracy, and depth of insights provided by their solution have been a game-changer for our analytics operations.” — Kratik Malhotra , Coach AFT

Conclusion

Akoode Technologies’ innovative computer vision solution has set a new benchmark for football analytics, delivering real-time, precise, and actionable insights. This project highlights Akoode’s ability to combine cutting-edge technology with practical applications, empowering clients to stay ahead in a competitive field.

Case Study: Securing and Managing Digital Infrastructure for Sarita Handa Group

Client: Sarita Handa Group

Company Size - 1000+ Employees

Company Revenue - 80+ M Dollors

Industry: Luxury Home Furnishings & Textiles

Services Provided: Cybersecurity, Website Recovery, Web Application & Server Management

Challenge:

Sarita Handa Group, a leading luxury home furnishings brand, faced a severe cyberattack that compromised their website, internal systems, and critical web applications. The breach resulted in website downtime, data vulnerabilities, and operational disruptions, impacting customer trust and online business functions.

The company needed:
  • Immediate threat mitigation and website recovery to restore business continuity.
  • Long-term security measures to prevent future attacks.
  • Efficient management of their web applications and servers to ensure optimal performance and security.
Akoode’s Solution:

We approached the project in a three-phase recovery and management strategy:

Emergency Cybersecurity Response & Website Recovery
  • Conducted an in-depth forensic analysis to assess the attack's impact and identify vulnerabilities.
  • Restored the website and core applications from secure backups while implementing stronger authentication measures.
  • Deployed enterprise-grade firewall protection and real-time malware detection to prevent further breaches.
  • Implemented SSL encryption and secure access protocols to enhance data security.
Server Management & Performance Optimization
  • Took over complete server management, ensuring high availability, stability, and security.
  • Migrated critical workloads to a secure cloud-based infrastructure, reducing downtime risks.
  • Set up automated security patches, backups, and performance monitoring to keep systems running smoothly.
  • Optimized server configurations to enhance speed, reliability, and scalability for future growth.
Web Application Management & Ongoing Maintenance
  • Assumed responsibility for multiple business-critical web applications, ensuring they function without disruptions.
  • Implemented AI-driven monitoring tools for proactive issue detection and response.
  • Provided continuous performance optimization and regular security updates.
  • Established 24/7 monitoring and incident response for quick resolutions.
Results & Impact:
  • 100% website uptime after full recovery and management.
  • Zero security breaches post-recovery due to enhanced cybersecurity measures.
  • 30% improvement in web application performance after server optimization.
  • Seamless transition with minimal downtime, ensuring business continuity.
  • Proactive monitoring and issue resolution, preventing future risks.
